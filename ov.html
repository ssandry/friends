Cinnamoroll (シナモロール, Shinamorōru) is a Sanrio character who was born on March 6, 2002, on a cloud far up in the sky. He is a little chubby male puppy with white fur, blue eyes, pink cheeks, a plump and curly tail that resembles a cinnamon roll, and long ears that enable him to fly. He made his debut in the manga Fluffy, Fluffy Cinnamoroll.
